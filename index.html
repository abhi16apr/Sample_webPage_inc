INCEDO



Incedo was founded in the year 2012 by Saurabh Mittal and Tejandra Pal Singh. Initally, it was called IB Tech Solutions, but then the name was changed to Incedo in the year 2015. Since 2017, the company has been headed over by the CEO Nitin Seth. The company is a digital transformation company who helps clients solving solutions through digital domains. The domains comprise of verticals and horizontals. The vertical domains are: Digital Engineering, Business Operations, life science and other domains.

There are horizontal domains which operate over all the other vertical domains present. Ashish Gupta heads Data and Ai, Arvind Ramamurthy heads Life Scienes department, Pamela Sharma heads the business operations, etc. There are eight core integral values that the company follows to ensure the success it has today in terms of growth and client relationships across all the domains.
